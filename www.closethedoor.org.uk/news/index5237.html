CtD Twitter campaign - please do your bit

Posted by admin on January 8, 2010

Our Twitter campaign has taken off and has been taken up by the 10:10 campaign, the Guardian and other environmental commentators. Join the debate at www.twitter.com to spread the word or re-tweet (RT) existing messages that can be found by searching on #closethedoor. Anyone not already using twitter can go to Youtube and the short Twitter in Plain English clip.
