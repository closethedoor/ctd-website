Youngest ever CtD representative gets Zac Goldsmith's endorsement

Posted by admin on February 8, 2013

Raffi (pictured) and his dad Jules Harley have managed to secure Zac Goldsmith's backing for Close the Door!

Zac is the Conservative MP for Richmond Park and North Kingston as well as being one of the UK's leading writers and speakers on the environment. He was a longstanding editor-in-chief of the Ecologist magazine and recently published a book "The Constant Economy", on environment problems and a programme of actions for tackling them. He has won international awards for environmental leadership.

Good Housekeeping, Jan 2013

Posted by admin on January 14, 2013

Good Housekeeping featured us in their January Edition, writing:

Shut that door!

Why do some shops insist on heating the High Street by needlessly pumping hot air on to their open doorways? Research from Cambridge University shows that closing the door when using heating or air-con can save up to 50% of a shop's total carbon emissions. The Close The Door campaign has big name retailers, including John Lewis, M&S and Tesco, behind it - and now they want us to help. 'Consumers can close shop doors behind them, write to head offices, or go to closethedoor.org.uk,' says spokesman Rachel Lankester.
